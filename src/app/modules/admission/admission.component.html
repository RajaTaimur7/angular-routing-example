Admission Procedure - UET Peshawar

Admission to the University of Engineering and Technology (UET) Peshawar is highly competitive and based on merit. Here's an overview of the admission procedure:

1. Eligibility Criteria:

Applicants must meet the eligibility criteria set by UET Peshawar. This typically includes minimum educational qualifications and prerequisites for the desired program.

2. Entry Test:

All candidates must appear in the UET Peshawar Entry Test. The test assesses candidates' knowledge in relevant subjects and is an important part of the admission process.

3. Submission of Application:

After the entry test, candidates should submit their applications online through the official UET Peshawar website. The application form will require personal and academic information.

4. Merit List:

UET Peshawar will prepare a merit list based on the entry test results and academic qualifications. The merit list will determine the admission of candidates into various programs.

5. Interview (if required):

Some programs may require candidates to appear for an interview or group discussion as part of the selection process.

6. Fee Payment:

Candidates who are selected for admission must pay the required admission and tuition fees within the specified deadline to secure their seat.

7. Orientation:

After successfully securing admission, students are invited to attend an orientation program to familiarize themselves with the university's policies, campus, and academic life.

8. Commencement of Classes:

Classes for the new academic session commence as per the university's academic calendar.
